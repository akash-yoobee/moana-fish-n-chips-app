Moana Fish & Chips

From the day we opened our shop in 1977 we have tried to do the right thing.

  • • We use free range eggs in all our cooking.
  • • All our beef comes from grass fed cows.
  • • We use locally sourced ingredients wherever possible.
  • • All our vegetables are organic and 100% GM free.
  • • We use recycled packaging which is also compostable after disposal.
  • • The ink we use on our packaging is made from a natural vegetable oil base sourced from renewable resources, instead of the usual mineral oil solvent stuff.
  • • All our aioli and ketchup punnets are made from corn starch which means they are plastic free and 100% compostable.
  • • Wherever possible we take the old oil from our friers and turn it into Bio Diesel.
