Here are some of our business practices

We use recycled packaging which is also compostable after disposal

The ink we use on our packaging is made from a natural vegetable oil base sourced from renewable resources, instead of the usual mineral oil solvent stuff.

Wherever possible we take the old oil from their friers and turn it into Bio Diesel.

All Aioli Punnets in the shop are made from corn starch which means they are plastic free and 100% compostable.
